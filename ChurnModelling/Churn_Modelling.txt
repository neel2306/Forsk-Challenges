The customer churn, also known as customer attrition, refers to the phenomenon whereby a customer leaves a service provider.


It is advantageous for banks to know what leads a client towards the decision to leave the company.

Churn prevention allows companies to develop loyalty programs and retention campaigns to keep as many customers as possible.

In this code challenge, we use customer data from a bank to construct a predictive model using deep learning for the likely churn customers.
